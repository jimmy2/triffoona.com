## History

Originating as a small-scale vegetable farm, we've grown into a full-scale operation located in the scenic area of Chelsea, Melbourne. Oonagability Triffeliz, our founder, comes from a lineage of Italian farmers who firmly believed in providing their community with fresh, healthy produce. With years of experience in agriculture and a passion for organic and sustainable farming, Oonagability turned her vision into reality, establishing a business that has gained recognition for delivering some of the best vegetables in Melbourne.
